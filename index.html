Advanced Wind Energy Technology Research Group

VAWT-X Energy is a start-up, limited liability company headquartered in South Australia. Our mission is to revolutionize wind energy by developing and commercializing cutting-edge 2-bladed vertical axis wind turbine (VAWT) technology.

Our Aims

To revolutionize wind energy by developing and commercializing cutting-edge 2-bladed vertical axis wind turbine (VAWT) technology. Our innovative approach combines advanced materials science with computational fluid dynamics to create more efficient and sustainable wind energy solutions.

Research Focus Areas

Technical Capabilities

  • Experimental aerodynamics
  • Computational fluid dynamics
  • Structural analysis
  • Material science
  • Energy systems optimization

Application Areas

  • Urban wind energy solutions
  • Offshore wind farms
  • Hybrid renewable systems
  • Energy storage integration
  • Smart grid technologies

Research Projects

Urban VAWT Deployment

Developing vertical axis turbines optimized for urban environments with turbulent wind conditions.

Offshore Applications

Researching corrosion-resistant materials and designs for offshore wind energy.

Hybrid Systems

Integrating wind energy with solar and storage for reliable renewable power.
